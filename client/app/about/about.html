About Prosperence


Mission

Prosperence is a personal financial planning application designed to change the world of personal finance. Prosperence provides a platform designed to empower and educate users so that everyone can have a long-term financial plan.

Whether on your own, or with the help of a financial advisor, Prosperence provides the tools and resources you need build a comprehensive financial plan that you can be confident in.


Drive

It's no secret that most people don't have an adequate financial plan, or any financial plan at all. In my time as a financial advisor, I got to see, first hand, the great need for a better personal planning service.

From paying off debt to saving for retirement, and everything inbetween, Prosperence offers a safe, no-pressure environment to help you build your plan and achieve your financial goals in a couple different ways:

  1. You can work through the Plan Builder to create or start building your financial plan independently.
  2. You can look for and connect with personal financial adivsors. Compare fee structures, stragies, reviews, and more to find an advisor that's right for you.


Details

Prosperence takes an innovative approach to personal financial planning by work with users in an intuitive and education-focused way to dispel the uncertainty and fear of finance, and instead, build confidence and independence.

It also provide a platform where you can search, compare, and communicate with financial advisors in a no-pressure environment.

Prosperence is an open source project hosted on GitHub. Check it out, tell us what you think, share your expertise, and help make the world a better place.

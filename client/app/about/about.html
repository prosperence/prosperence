About Prosperence


Mission

Prosperence is a personal financial planning application designed to change the world of personal finance by providing the tools and resources to empower users to develop a plan independently and confidently.


Drive

In my time as a financial advisor, I saw both the great need for personal planning services as well as the flaws of the current financial planning industry. People don’t know what to do, they don’t know where to start, and can’t trust financial advisors.

From paying off debt to saving for retirement, and everything inbetween, Prosperence offers a safe, no-pressure environment to help you achieve your financial goals.


Details

Prosperence takes an innovative approach to personal financial planning by work with users in an intuitive and education-focused way to dispel the uncertainty and fear of finance, and instead, build confidence and independence.

Prosperence is an open source project hosted on GitHub. Check it out, tell us what you think, share your expertise, and help make the world a better place.
